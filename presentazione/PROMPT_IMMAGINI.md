## 🖼️ IMG 1 — Immagine Hero (sfondo principale)
**Nome file: `img-hero.jpg`**
**Dimensioni consigliate: 1920×1080 px (landscape, molto orizzontale)**

**Prompt per ChatGPT / Grok:**
```
Vast flat agricultural plain (Po Valley, Romagna, northern Italy) at night. Dense, low-lying fog drifts slowly across dark harvested fields. In the extreme far distance, barely visible through the mist, a tiny dark hooded silhouette stands motionless. The sky is deep blue-black with no moon. The fog glows faintly with diffuse cold light from no visible source. Ultra-wide cinematic composition, photorealistic, deeply atmospheric. Color palette: near-black earth, cold white-grey fog, deep blue sky, no warm tones. Ground level shot. Inspired by the visual style of Robert Eggers' The Witch (2015) and Andrei Tarkovsky. No text, no watermarks.
```

**Variante se il prompt non funziona:**
```
Cinematic wide-angle photograph: flat misty fields at night in rural northern Italy. Dense ground fog. Dark silhouette of a hooded figure barely visible far away. Cold, desolate atmosphere. No people in foreground. Ultra-dark, photorealistic. 16:9 aspect ratio.

## 🖼️ IMG 3 — Rinaldo (Protagonista)
**Nome file: `img-rinaldo.jpg`**
**Dimensioni consigliate: 900×1200 px (portrait, verticale)**

**Prompt per ChatGPT / Grok:**
```
A lone male figure standing in dense fog, seen from a medium distance (three-quarter shot, full body visible). He wears rough early 16th-century Italian peasant and physician clothing: coarse linen shirt, worn leather vest, a heavy cloak, leather satchel at his side containing herbs and medical tools. He faces slightly away from the viewer, looking into the fog ahead. His posture is tense but still — a man searching, not fleeing. His hands are precise and careful, the hands of a healer, not a fighter. Atmospheric, painterly style inspired by Caravaggio's chiaroscuro and Jean-François Millet. Color palette: dark earth browns, grey fog, muted warm skin tones barely visible. No fantasy elements. No text, no watermarks.
```

**Dimensioni consigliate: 900×1200 px (portrait, verticale)**

**Prompt per ChatGPT / Grok:**
```
A distant, solitary hooded figure standing completely motionless in a foggy field at night. The figure is far away, appearing small against the expanse of fog and dark sky — barely more than a silhouette. It is entirely covered in dark, heavy fabric, no features visible.
