# PROMPT — Video campanellino
### Un video da mettere in loop permanente sotto la card del campanellino

Genera con **Grok Video** (o Kling / RunwayML).
Durata: **5–6 secondi**, loop seamless (inizio e fine identici).
Salva in `presentazione/assets/gallery/` come **`bell.mp4`**.

---

## PROMPT

```
A small worn hand bell (campanellino) sitting alone on a dark rough wooden surface.
The bell is simple iron or bronze, domestic, used — not decorative.
A slow warm golden-amber glow emanates from inside the bell and pulses softly:
growing brighter, then dimming, then brightening again — one full pulse in 5 seconds.
The bell sways or vibrates very slightly as the glow intensifies, as if reacting
to something invisible nearby. The glow casts soft warm light on the dark wooden
surface around the base of the bell. The surrounding space remains in deep shadow.
The video starts and ends at exactly the same state for a perfect seamless loop.
Dark background. The only light source is the glow from inside the bell.
Cinematic, photorealistic, painterly style inspired by Caravaggio dark still life.
No text, no watermarks, no people.
```

---

## NOTE
- Il loop deve essere seamless: l'ultimo frame = il primo frame
- Il bagliore deve essere **lento e inquietante**, non festoso
- La vibrazione del campanello deve essere quasi impercettibile — suggerita, non esplicita
- Se Grok aggiunge altri oggetti in scena, rigenera: il campanellino deve essere solo

Salva come `bell.mp4` in `assets/gallery/`.
